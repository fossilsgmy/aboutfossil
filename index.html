FAQ

Q) IS THIS AN OFFICIAL FOSSIL STORE?
 A) Yes, this is an official Fossil store. All products sold here are authentic.

Q) WILL YOUR PRODUCT COME WITH WARRANTY?
 A) Fossil watch is warranted for a period two (2) years from the original date of purchase under the terms and conditions of the warranty. A copy of an official sales receipt or our authorised dealer stamp in the warranty booklet is required as a proof of purchase.

Q) WHERE DO I SEND MY WATCH FOR REPAIR?
 A) You may visit Fossil Singapore Service Centre for servicing. Address: 51 Bras Basah Rd, #08-07 Manulife Centre, 189554

Q) WILL MY PRODUCT COME WITH A FOSSIL TIN BOX?
 A) Only the purchase of Fossil watches will come together with tin boxes (excluding display smartwatches and hybrid smartwatches where original packaging will be used). No tin box will be given in the case of leather goods purchase.

Q) ABOUT WATER RESISTANT
 A) See caseback for water resistance rating for traditional watches and Hybrid Smartwatches. For Display Smartwatches visit fossilgroup.com/water for water resistance information.

The water resistance rating provides guidelines for appropriate water-related exposure to avoid damage to internal watch components. Please see chart for recommended usage guidelines.

Q) ABOUT FOSSIL TIN BOX
 Every purchase of Fossil watch through the official store will come together with a tin box (excluding display smartwatch and hybrid smartwatch). Tin box varies by design and is subject to stock availability.
ABOUT FOSSIL
Fossil is an American watch and lifestyle company, creatively rooted in authentic vintage and classic design. We strive to create high-quality watches, bags, jewellery and more that preserve the best of the past while updating it for today. When the clean lines of the mid-century meet the modern looks of today, the result is key items that represent the best of what we stand for: bags that pair portability with streamlined design, traditional watches created in fresh hues and materials, and timeless clothes and accessories that accompany you wherever you are traveling.
